Delbridge, Rick & Lowe, James & Oliver, Nick. (1995). The Process of Benchmarking: A Study from the Automotive Industry. International Journal of Operations & Production Management. 15. 50-62. 10.1108/01443579510083604. Benchmarking is a topic which is currently attracting considerable
interest among both academics and practitioners in the manufacturing
community. Yet little has been written about the assumptions which lie
behind benchmarking. As a process, benchmarking remains poorly
understood. Describes a benchmarking study in the auto components
industry, and makes a preliminary attempt to identify the principles of
good practice in benchmarking.
